About

Catastrophe was an ambitious idea from the start. We had no idea what we were getting ourselves into however, we were committed until the end. For an introduction to Game Development class, this projects highlights what we have learned throughout the semester.

Start up a game with up to 12 friends on the same network. One player must act as a host connection, while the others wait to connect.
